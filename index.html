Destairty

Swift-Smart-Swave-Sharp

Destarity group of companies, aims at creating an entertainment ecosystem for the budding talents. A training hub for the aspirants, casting in the field of entertainment, events as well as public relationship management for Personnel.

Why Is It Special

Way2star

Way2star is an unique and innovative way to portray one's talent in the field of entertainment. It has been created with an aim of giving better and equipped training to nurture the building talents. All round development of an individual includes co-curricular activities and enhancing the same is our motto. W2S is creating a perfect market place for such activities. Get trained, grab opportunity and reach your goal.

Way2fest

Way2fest was created by pairing together our passion for business and events. We bring a fresh, unique approach to the event management industry. Our team understands that a properly executed event can be leveraged to support an organisation's strategic vision, incorporated into a country's marketing plan, or used to build networks and client loyality.

Way2knot

Way2knot is a full-service PR and communication agency delivering a comprehensive range of integrated marketing services to clients, and our practice areas are not siloed or segregated. From consumers product goods PR and corporate communications to hospitality, health, and food, our team implements thoughtful, strategic, passionate, and powerful public relations for every industry.
